Potato Salad

Description

Tender potatoes tossed in a bacon vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups diced potatoes
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 1 small onion
  • 1/4 cup white vinager
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar, to taste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes until just cooked enough to easily pierce with fork.
  2. Fry bacon until browned and crispy. Keep bacon grease. Transfer bacon to plate to cool.
  3. Once bacon is cool, break into pieces.
  4. Cook diced onion in the bacon grease until browned.
  5. Add vinager, water, sugar, salt, and pepper, and bring to boil.
  6. Toss the potatoes in the dressing.
  7. Mix in the bacon pieces and parsley, and serve.
