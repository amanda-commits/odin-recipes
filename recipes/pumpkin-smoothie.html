Pumpkin Smoothie

Description

Creamy, pumpkin pie flavoured smoothie, to enjoy with an autumn mood.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • pinch of each of
    • nutmeg
    • ginger
    • allspice
    • cloves
    • black pepper

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into glass, and garnish with chopped pecans if desired.
