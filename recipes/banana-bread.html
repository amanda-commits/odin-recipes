Banana Bread

Description

A cake made with overripe bananas. Easy to make, and tasty to eat for a snack anytime of day!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups mashed overripe banana

Directions

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, salt into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Beat in brown sugar and butter, mix until smooth.
  3. Stir in beaten eggs and mashed banana until just combined.
  4. Pour batter into greased 9x5 loaf pan.
  5. Bake at 350 deg F for about 60 min.
